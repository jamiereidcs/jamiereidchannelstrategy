What's Inside

Six Pillars

Every layer of partner marketing execution, from strategic alignment to ROI measurement.

01

Foundation

Strategic Alignment

Define ICP, priority verticals, geographies, buyer personas, and the role partners play in your go-to-market motion.

02

Messaging

Positioning & Messaging

Build the messaging foundation partners need: elevator pitches, persona-based messaging, case studies, co-branding guidelines, and differentiation.

03

Enablement

Competitive Readiness

Equip partners with battlecards, objection handling, pricing guidance, and competitive differentiation messaging to win against alternatives.

04

Execution

Campaign Execution Model

Lay out the campaign model: co-branded assets, targeting strategy, conversion targets, and program incentives for high-performing partners.

05

Intelligence

AI-Enabled Targeting

Leverage AI for ICP account identification, partner-to-account matching, personalized outreach, and campaign performance modeling.

06

Measurement

ROI & Funnel Metrics

Measure the full funnel: pipeline generation, meeting velocity, conversion performance, revenue impact, and AI-driven targeting performance.
